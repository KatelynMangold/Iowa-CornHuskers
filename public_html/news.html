Iowa Cornhuskers 2018 News

Game Recap

The Cornhuskers started off their 2018 campaign with a resounding win against the Orange-Peelers.
From the first kickoff, the Cornhuskers completely dominated the game in all facets. QB Ball Thrower
was spectacular, going 27/29 for 674 yards and 9 touchdowns in the first quarter! WR Speedy McNeil was
also in his bag, with an astounding 6 receptions for 315 yards and 3 punt return touchdowns. Hard Head
Sr. wrecked havoc on the opposing D-Line per usual with 8 sacks and 1 fumble forced. However,
the Cornhuskers received a huge blow to their #1 ranked defense, losing stud DB Chris DaBlack for
the season in the 2nd quarter. Look for Sam Adeniy to step up and fill the void DaBlack leaves.

Next Game

The Cornhuskers play the Taxi - Drivers, a perennial bottom feeder in the EBG conference. Expect OC Puff to run up the score once again.
